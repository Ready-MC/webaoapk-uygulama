Missions of Love

In an effort to prove that love can bloom even on the battlefield, we've prepared a bouquet of missions to celebrate your camaraderie and cooperation!

A few quick notes before we get into the good stuff:

1. The missions inside each chain are sequentially linked, meaning you'll have to complete your current mission in that chain to get to the next one.

2. They're also time-locked, as indicated by the Day column. Even should you complete your current missions, you'll only have access up to the current day. That means Day 1 missions will be available on 2/12/2021 (when the Lunar Lovers event starts), Day 2 missions on 2/13/2021, Day 3 on 2/14/2021, and so on.

3. Missions that include players in your party are referring to premade groups, so find some friends and queue up for quick rewards!

Got it? Good! Let's talk missions!
